Apeiron

Eylon Kadish (Israel) AKA Apeiron, PsykoRhythm (Ex. Kymatica, GoAtma, GoAtmika) Goa Trance and Electronic producer since 2012 A new perspective of retro Goa Trance, with many collaborations and projects Eylon tries to find the future sound of Goa, testing the boundaries of sound design and melodies.
