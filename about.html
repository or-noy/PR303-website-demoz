About 

us




Parallel Records 303 is a Goa Trance and Psychedelic Trance label based in Israel.

The label was founded by Yarden Harari and Elad Cohen in 2018 under the agenda of releasing and supporting new and old goa trance music.

In 2020 Elad Cohen has left the label and in 2021 Eylon Kadish and Or Noy has became co owners of the label together with Yarden Harari.

The label is representing some of the most talented and creative Goa Trance artists in Israel.
